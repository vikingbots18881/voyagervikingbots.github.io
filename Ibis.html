Ibis is a drawing app with over 200 brushes, Can you believe it? With this mass variety of brushes, You can make a lot of detailed art. To add to that, You can also import photos and images. You can even share your drawings to friends and family, Or publish them! The top 10 creations every day are shown in the front screen, Meaning you could get 1st! You can create your own colors and save them between drawings, Letting you quickly get them. Thats it! Try the app for yourself, See if you like it! (By Donovan White)
