This page will show you what the Bing AI Creator App is and how to use it. To start, you have to go to the App Store on your mobile device and serch "Bing" and download it.

Next, when you are on the Bing app, if you have a "sign in" screen on your device, you need a Microsoft account. If you don't have a Microsoft account or don't know what it is, you can go to this link.

When you are signed in, you will be on the homepage with many options. Then, scroll over until you see the paintbrush and picture icon. Click on it.

Once you have clicked the "Image Creator" icon, it will bring you to a new page. On this page, you will see a lot of pictures, those are what pepole have already made. Click the black text bar and type what you want it to look like.

The lightning bolts right by the text bar in a yellow circle, those are called boosts. Boosts make you wait less for your picture to be ready, but you can still genorate pictuers without boosts, but it will take longer.

Now you know the basics about Bing AI Image Creator! Use your imagination to generate any picture! Before you leave, you can check out our wall of inspiration and also tips and tricks!
