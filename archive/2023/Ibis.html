Ibis paint X


An introduction to Ibis

ibisPaint x is a wonderful drawing app, you import a picture and start drawing!

Ibis has 385 brushes, Can you believe it?


With this mass variety of brushes, You can make a lot of detailed art.

You can even share your drawings to friends and family, Or publish them!

The top 20 creations every day are shown in the front screen, Meaning you could get 1st!


You can create your own colors and save them between drawings, Letting you quickly get them.

You can blur, special effect, erase, eyedrop(color copying tool) and brush of course!

To open better brushes you can get premium which you have to pay every month for, or watch an add to-get them free for 18 hours! Which opens much more creativity!

You can layer,and have basically outlines for stuff that could colored or not colored.

You can make very detailed and defined pictures and I hope this app opens many more creativity tabs for you to take.


Thats it! Try the app for yourself, See if you like it!
